# CSE-3902-Legendary-Legend-of-Zelda-Dungeon
Team Project for CSE 3902, creating the first dungeon of Legend of Zelda.

## Sprint2 Controls
---
### General
|Action | Control |
|:-|:-:|
|Quit Game | Q|
|Reset Game | R|
---
### Link
|Action |Control   |
|:-|:-:|
|Walk Up | W |
|Walk Down | S |
|Walk Left | A |
|Walk Right | D |
|Use Sword | Z or N |
|Damage Link | E |
|Pick Up Heart Container | 1 |
|Pick Up Triforce Piece | 2 |
|Pick Up Bow | 3 |
|Use Bow | 4 |
|Pick Up Boomerang | 5 |
|Use Boomerang | 6 |
|Use Bomb | 7 |
|Use Sword Beam | 8 | 
---
### Switching Between Blocks, Enemies, and Items
|Action | Control |
|:-|:-:|
|Previous Block | T |
|Next Block | Y |
|Previous Item | U |
|Next Item | I |
|Previous Block | O |
|Next Block | P |
