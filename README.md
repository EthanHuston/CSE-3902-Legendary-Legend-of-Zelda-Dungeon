## Controls
### General
|Action | Control |
|:-|:-:|
|Quit Game | Q |
|Reset Game | R |
|Switch Room | Click on door with LMB |

### Link
|Action |Control   |
|:-|:-:|
|Walk Up | W |
|Walk Down | S |
|Walk Left | A |
|Walk Right | D |
|Use Sword | Z or N |
|Damage Link | E |
|Pick Up Heart Container | 1 |
|Pick Up Triforce Piece | 2 |
|Pick Up Bow | 3 |
|Use Bow | 4 |
|Pick Up Boomerang | 5 |
|Use Boomerang | 6 |
|Use Bomb | 7 |
|Use Sword Beam | 8 |
