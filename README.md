## Controls
### Main Menu
|Action | Control |
|:-|:-:|
|Quit Game | Esc |
|Start Game | LMB |

### In Game
|Action |Control   |
|:-|:-:|
|Walk Up | W or Up Arrow |
|Walk Down | S or Down Arrow |
|Walk Left | A or Left Arrow |
|Walk Right | D or Right Arrow |
|Use Primary | LMB or Q |
|Use Secondary | RMB or E |
|Open Inventory | Tab |
|Change Secondary | LMB in Inventory |
|Pause | Esc |
